About Me

What makes me unique as a data analyst is my ability to transform complex datasets into clear, actionable insights. I don't just analyze data — I focus on understanding the business problem behind it. I combine strong SQL skills, data cleaning precision, and interactive dashboard design to deliver insights that support real decision-making.

With a Bachelor's degree in Law from Helwan University and a Postgraduate Diploma in Economics from Ain Shams University, I bring a unique interdisciplinary perspective to data analysis. My background in law developed my analytical thinking and research capabilities, while my economics training strengthened my data interpretation and economic analysis skills.

Currently, I'm focused on advancing my career in data analysis, having completed the Digital Egypt Pioneers Initiative (DEPI) program. I excel at translating business needs into data solutions and bridging the gap between technical analysis and business strategy.
